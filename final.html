OUR MISSION

About Scholarship Summit

Scholarship Summit is dedicated to empowering students across India by providing transparent, up-to-date information on scholarships, eligibility, and application processes. Our mission is to bridge the gap between deserving students and life-changing opportunities.

Our Portals

  • Helping Hand: A platform for students to share notes, donate books, and build a collaborative learning community.
  • Mentor-Mentee: Connects experienced professionals with students for guidance and mentorship opportunities.
  • Scholarship Summit: Provides a comprehensive tool to calculate scholarship eligibility based on key criteria.
